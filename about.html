ImprovedMind was inspired through a series of discussions with family, friends and acquaintances on the topic of meditation. A reoccurring question raised - the question on how to get started with the practice of meditation. This is the reason why this collection of resources was created. To help all of you wonderful humans explore the beautiful world of meditation through books, blogs, apps and people.

Alexander Traykov is a product designer born, raised and based in Sofia, Bulgaria.
